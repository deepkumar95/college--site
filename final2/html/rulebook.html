GENERAL RULES FOR THE EVENTS:

  • Sports: Single entry per event, per institute, however the host institute may have 2 entries.
  • Cultural: The guest colleges can register two entries for all the cultural events except for the Fashion Show which will have only one entry.
  • At least one faculty coordinator and two student coordinators from the institute should be present in the campus throughout the events.
  • Participants should carry their college identity cards & TWO STAMP-sized photographs with them.
  • Practice room will be provided, if intimated well in advance.
  • Each event may have its own special rules. Please refer to the event page for more information.
  • The Institute reserves the right to cancel or reschedule any event without any prior information.
  • Any kind of damage to college property will not be entertained and those responsible will be penalized.
  • The host institute will not provide any TA
  • The decision of the jury will be final.
  • For the information regarding the time and venue of the events, the institute coordinator must be in contact with the organizing committee.
  • The participants must be present at the venue at the stipulated time
  • A waiting time of maximum 2mins would be given.
  • No entry for non-participating students from invited colleges.
  • Drinking and smoking is strictly prohibited in the college campus.
